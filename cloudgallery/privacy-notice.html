Privacy Notice

Last modified on July 11, 2020

The Privacy Policy describes how we treat personal information when you use the Cloud Gallery App. This privacy notice describes privacy practices that are specific to third-party cloud accounts supported by the app.

Here is a list of third-party cloud accounts supported by the app with their respective privacy policy linked:

  • Google account
  • Flickr account
What information is collected?

In addition to the information listed in the Privacy Policy, the app also collects the following:

When you sign in with your cloud account, you are creating a local account in your device that is associated with your cloud account. The information collected includes: name, email, profile picture, photo library metadata and access token needed to retrieve updated content. While the app remains opened and if necessary, it will periodically try to refresh the access token in case it gets expired.

You will be able to manually trigger the library sync process at any time by pressing the sync button within the app. This information will be retained on your device until you remove the account from the accounts settings screen or uninstall the app.

How is the information used?

In addition to the uses listed in the Privacy Policy, the information that you provided is used so you can:

  • Easily identify your account - The app displays your username or email and profile picture to ensure that you're aware of which account is being used and clearly distinguish between other authenticated accounts. You can change the displayed name from the account's settings screen at any time.
  • Browse your photo library - Your photo library is displayed in an organized way allowing you to easily browse it's content such as albums, photos and videos.
  • Set up a screensaver - Depending on the version of the app and the device you're on, you might be able to setup the screen saver feature to show your albums when the tv goes into standby mode.
How is the information stored?
  • Account information: For security reasons this information is stored encrypted on your device. The information includes: name, email, profile picture and access token required to access the photo library.
  • Photo library metadata: This information is stored in a private database on your device's internal memory. This information is required to retrieve your library content such as albums, photos and videos. The information includes: id, title, type, description and urls needed to display the content.
  • Media preview: Photos and videos can be cached temporarily by the app in order to enhance the user experience. This data gets cleared after a short period of time and will never be stored permanently.
Will your information be shared with anyone?

The personal information you provide to the app is stored locally on your device and will not be shared with anyone. However, the app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. For a full list of service providers used by the app, please see the main Privacy Policy

Changes to this Privacy Notice

We may update our Privacy Notice from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Notice on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
